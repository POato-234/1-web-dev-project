Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played by two teams, typically consisting of five players on each side.
The objective of the game is to score points by shooting the ball through the opposing team's hoop or basket,
which is a netted structure positioned 10 feet above the ground.The game is played on a rectangular court,
with teams trying to move the ball towards the basket by dribbling (bouncing the ball while moving)
or passing it to teammates. The opposing team tries to block the ball or prevent scoring by playing defense.
